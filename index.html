Capture What Matters

We believe memories are more than just photos—they’re stories, emotions, and experiences. Recollect is designed to help you preserve them authentically with tools that keep your moments meaningful.

No Vanity Metrics: No likes, no followers—just real moments.

Photo Journaling: Add captions, audio, and reflections.

Delayed Gratification: Photos develop after 24-48 hours to help you reflect and appreciate.

Memories With Purpose

Recollect isn’t just about taking photos—it’s about cherishing them. We provide tools to help you revisit your life’s best moments.

Unlimited Storage: Save all your moments without worrying about space.

Collaborative Albums: Build shared albums with friends and family.

Digital Photo Wall: Your personal space for life’s milestones and adventures.

Your Memories, Your Way

At Recollect, we’re all about putting you in control of your moments. Keep them private, share with friends, or print your favorites—it’s your choice.

Custom Privacy Controls: Decide who sees what.

A Journaling Experience: Combine visuals and storytelling to create something truly personal.

Multi Device Access: We are working on making it accessible from your computer.
